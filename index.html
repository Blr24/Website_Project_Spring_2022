Integrator · Explorer · Tech, Space, & Entertanment Enthusiast

Hey there, I'm Brice and I'm currently enrolled full-time at the Georgetown University McDonough School of Business where I'm working towards earning my Master in Business Administration with concentrations in STEM and sustainable business. Before re-entering the academic world as a student for the first time in eight years this past fall, I was working in the Greater Washington Area as an IT implementation consultant for a couple firms in the area. Before moving to DC, I lived in Mantes-la-Jolie, France (just northwest of Paris) teaching English in a French public school for one year, following completion of my undergraduate degree, which I received from Carnegie Mellon University in 2012. My life to this point has been a dynamic mix of people, places, and interests - from growing up in the US, Central America, and Europe with the military community, to traveling far and wide meeting people and experiencing life different than the way I have. Hopefully this site will give you a better glimpse into my professional and personal interests and allow you to get to know me better. Feel free to visit the Contact Me page to send me a message!
